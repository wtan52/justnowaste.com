Tyre

A tyre is a thick piece of rubber which is fitted onto the wheels of vehicles such as cars, buses, and bicycles.

Recyclable

-Rubber waste can be recycled at household recyclable centres or nearby tyre service centres to recycle the tyres.

Hazardous substances

Tyre waste leads to breeding ground for mosquitoes.

Impact on Environment

Rubber waste has fire hazard and a breeding ground for disease through mosquitoes, and that has a flow-on effect for both vegetation and wildlife.

Rubber mats

A rubber mat is a generic term for a piece of flat rubber, generally placed on a floor or other flat surface

Recyclable

-Rubber waste can be recycled at household recyclable centres or nearby tyre service centres to recycle the tyres.

Hazardous substances

Rubber mats contain benzene, toluene, arsenic, and acetone.

Impact on Environment

Rubber mats smell really bad when it is sent to landfill.
