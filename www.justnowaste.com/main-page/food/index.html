Apple

Residues left after being eaten or discarded fruits

Non-recyclable

-Red-lid wheelie bin

Hazardous substances

Fruit-based garbage is not harmful, but organic matter oxidation, rot and other conditions will damage environmental sanitation, breeding a large number of mosquitoes, leading to the spread of disease.

Impact on Environment

If Fruit-based garbage is simply piled up, it will invade a large amount of land, organic matter will decompose to produce foul odor, and release a large amount of ammonia, sulfide and other pollutants into the atmosphere, as well as generate a large amount of methane, which is a highly harmful greenhouse gas, leadingg to global warming.

Milk

Milk is a nutrient-rich, white liquid food. In this category, milk will refer to daily drinks.

Non-recyclable

-Pour into the sewer

Hazardous substances

Liquid-based garbage is not harmful, but organic matter oxidation, rot and other conditions will damage environmental sanitation, breeding a large number of mosquitoes, leading to the spread of disease.

Impact on Environment

Unreasonable handling of liquid-based waste can lead to the spread of disease. Liquid-based garbage not only contains pathogenic microorganisms, but also provides food, habitat and breeding sites for mice, birds and mosquitoes, and is also the source of infectious diseases.

Pork rib

Pork ribs are a cut of pork popular in Western and Asian cuisines.

Non-recyclable

-Red-lid wheelie bin

Hazardous substances

Meat-based garbage is not harmful, but organic matter oxidation, rot and other conditions will damage environmental sanitation, breeding a large number of mosquitoes, leading to the spread of disease.

Impact on Environment

First, if animal food is thrown away, such as pork, it can have up to three times the climate impact of plant foods because it has released more greenhouse gases. The environmental costs of animal food are not limited to methane emissions. Producing food can have a negative impact on the environment – it is estimated that one-third of global carbon emissions come from agriculture. Secondly, the waste of meat from landfill disposal will also produce greenhouse gases, causing environmental pollution.
