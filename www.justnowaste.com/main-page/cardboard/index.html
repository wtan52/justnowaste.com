Shoe box

the carton used for containing shoes

Recyclable

-Yellow-lid wheelie bin

Hazardous substances

lead and other heavy metals

Impact on Environment

Carton products consume a large amount of raw materials in the production process, and it takes a lot of energy to process these raw materials into final products. on the other hand, Carton waste occupies space and land, destroying soil structure, leading to negative impacts such as crop yield reduction, etc.
In addition, the chlorofluorocarbons used in the production of carton wastes result in a reduction in the amount of ozone. And carton emits toxic and harmful substances in the production process, causing pollution to water, atmosphere and soil.

Hazardous substances

Ink, lead and other heavy metals

Impact on Environment

Paper bag contains some ink, lead and other harmful substances. When burning, some harmful substances on the shoe box will be discharged into the air along with the smoke, and will also produce a large amount of carbon monoxide and carcinogens 3,4-benzo, which pollute the air and endanger human health. After improper recovery and landfill, the organic matter in the shoe box will generate methane, causing the ozone layer to be hollow, and the waste water and exhaust gas generated will seriously pollute the environment.

Paper bag

A paper bag is a bag made of paper, commonly used as shopping bags, packaging, and sacks.

Recyclable

-Yellow-lid wheelie bin

Hazardous substances

lead and other heavy metals

Impact on Environment

If paper can be recycled properly, the process of recycled paper pulping will cause less pollution to the atmosphere, water quality, etc. than ordinary paper.Moreover, in the manufacturing process, recycled paper can reduce the discharge of wastewater by 50%, which not only saves the papermaking production process, but also does not cause pollution of black liquor, and greatly reduces the degree of pollution. This will reduce the negative impact of paper on the environment.
